Armando P. Ramirez

Le Wagon Student

Armando served as a patent examiner in the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) in Washington D.C. until 2009, where he earned accreditations for prior art search skills. He applied federal law to evaluate patent applications, and his work put him in close contact with patent agents and attorneys. Armando founded Youvention upon realizing that the process for filing patent applications was prohibitively complex and expensive for the independent inventor. Dr. Ramirez also worked as a scientific researcher at Boston College, Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory, and earned his doctorate degree from the University of California. He has published four scientific articles in prestigious peer-reviewed journals. In his spare time, he is an accomplished artist.
